What AI.MMUNIZED Stops

Built against the full OWASP LLM Top 10 and the most advanced real-world attack patterns targeting AI systems.

Direct Prompt Injection
Detected
Classic override attempts, role-play escapes, system prompt leakage, and direct jailbreak variants — caught before they reach your models.
Semantic Obfuscation
Detected
Encoding tricks, homoglyphs, indirect language, and metaphorical attack patterns analyzed at the semantic level, not just surface text.
Multi-Turn Attacks
Detected
Slow context manipulation across conversation turns tracked at the session level. Patterns that span messages don't escape correlation.
Agent Indirect Injection
Detected
Attacks embedded in tool outputs, retrieved documents, and external data sources inspected before they influence agent reasoning.
Low-and-Slow Recon
Detected
Distributed probing and slow behavioral drift detected by continuously updating baselines — not just snapshot rule matching.
PII & Data Leakage
Blocked
Credit cards, IDs, medical records, API keys, and passwords detected in both inputs and outputs, automatically redacted before exposure.
